The app

Fohr

On your phone. Always with you.

Type the words as they come. Fohr finds what you mean to say and gives you three ways to say it. No guessing. No pressure. Just your voice, surfaced.

  • Type words as they come and get three natural translations instantly
  • Draw what you mean when words are out of reach
  • Upload a photo and let Fohr help describe what you see
  • Builds a personal phrase library from your own words over time
  • Works on iPhone and Android

Fohr continually learns how best to support its users. The more you use it, the better it understands your voice, your patterns, and the way you mean things.
